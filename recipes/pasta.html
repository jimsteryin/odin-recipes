Cashew Cream Tomato Pasta!

Delicious vegan friendly pasta made with cashews!

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Pasta
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomato
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup basil leaves
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Cook Pasta according to package directions
  2. Soak Cashews for 2 hours
  3. Add cashews, tomatoes, garlic, basil to blender and blend until smooth
  4. Combine drained pasta and sauce in a pan
  5. Buon Appetito!
